Welcome to Fenjan!

Your beloved dinosaur that helps you to find your dream PhD positions

Fenaja is a tool designed to assist you in finding your dream Ph.D. position. We understand that searching through social media platforms for Ph.D. positions can be tedious and time-consuming. That's why we've developed Fenjan, it simplifies the process and saves your valuable time.

Developed by: Hue (MohammadHossein) Salari

Fenjan is expertly crafted to scour the latest posts and tweets on Twitter and LinkedIn, bringing you the most recent Ph.D. positions in your field. This ensures that you are always up-to-date and won't miss any opportunity.

The information is delivered to you via a user-friendly email feature, guaranteeing you have immediate access to the data you need, so you can focus on your research and leave the rest to us. We look forward to supporting you on your academic journey.
