Last night, I had the opportunity to sit down with John Morgan after speaking at ProductTank Chicago. We had a great discussion ranging from tech philosophy to practical differences between artificial intelligence up on the 51st floor of the Capital One building.

John's background is in Machine Learning so I appreciated having the chance to ask him which he felt was more valuable traditional ML or LLMs. His response from experience in both sides was great. Deterministic models will have a valuable place for years to come, especially in industries that need to provide the right answer. On the flip side, LLMs with their non-deterministic structure can be tremendous for creative fields and the many lower stakes consumer products.

A quick dialogue during a Q&A that should be a headline for any business leader pouring investment into Generative AI.
